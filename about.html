~About Us~

Originating as a wine retailer, we have now moved on to be a cafe orientated shop.  As it has always been, our motto is to provide you a luxury drinking experience.  Expect reliable and fast deliveries on the go, as well as having a dependable place to relax or work. To soothe all your needs, we provide a variety of products from drinks, snacks, and meals. Vivian aims to be the high end cafe that you need.  But why take our word when
you can first hand experience it!
